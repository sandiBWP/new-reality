# The New Reality Fund

The New Reality Fund is a professionally managed investment platform built to put capital back in the places people actually live.

Regional funds. National infrastructure. Real participation in the outcomes that matter to you — not just markets you'll never see.

---

## What It Is

- A platform for investing in private businesses and productive assets within specific regions
- Structured using Business Development Companies (BDCs) — a regulated vehicle created by Congress in 1980
- Centralized management ensures professional underwriting, compliance, and operations at scale
- Regional deployment ensures capital flows into the communities investors care about

## What It Is Not

- **Not Venture Capital** — not chasing unicorns in a handful of tech hubs
- **Not Private Equity** — not extracting value through leverage
- **Not ESG** — not screening public market portfolios for compliance
- **Not Philanthropy** — not grants or donations; investment with returns
- **Not Crowdfunding** — not fragmented, one-off deals

---

## The Structure

The NRF Management Company provides centralized oversight, strategy, and infrastructure — underwriting, compliance, technology, investor relations, distribution, operations, and marketing.

Regional BDCs operate under this umbrella. Each regional fund deploys capital into private businesses serving regional needs. The first deployment is planned for California, with additional regions to follow.

---

## Why Now

For the last forty years, capital was rewarded for becoming larger, more centralized, and more global. The objective was efficiency — not regional resilience.

As the importance of place increases, capital formation may need to become more regional as well. The opportunity is not to invent a new structure. It is to apply an existing structure to a changing reality.
