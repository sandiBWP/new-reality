# Investment Process

The path from "I'm interested" to "I'm invested" is designed to be thorough but straightforward.

---

## The Steps

**1. Express Interest**

Complete the Get Started form. You'll confirm your accredited investor status and tell us a bit about your interests.

**2. Accreditation Verification**

We verify accredited investor status as required by SEC regulations. This is a standard process for private investment offerings.

**3. Receive Documentation**

Once verified, you'll receive the fund's offering documents — including detailed information about investment objectives, risks, fees, and the specific regional funds available.

**4. Advisor Conversation**

A conversation with our team to discuss whether the fund aligns with your investment goals, risk tolerance, and overall portfolio strategy. This is not a sales call — it's a fit assessment.

**5. Subscription**

If the fund is a good fit, you'll complete subscription documents and fund your investment. Funds can typically be held in IRAs, brokerage accounts, and trust accounts.

**6. Ongoing Participation**

As an investor, you'll receive regular reporting on fund performance, regional deployment updates, and opportunities to participate in additional regional funds as they launch.

---

## Timeline

The process from initial expression of interest to completed investment typically takes 2-4 weeks, depending on documentation review and scheduling.

There is no obligation at any step in the process. The goal is to ensure that if you invest, you do so with full understanding and confidence.
